Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is the Falaj Management System?

The Falaj Management System is a platform designed to manage and optimize the distribution and maintenance of falaj irrigation systems, typically used in arid regions.

2. How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and follow the registration process. You will need to provide your name, email address, and create a password.

3. Is the Falaj Management System free to use?

Basic access to the Falaj Management System is free. However, we may offer premium features or subscription plans for advanced functionality.

4. How do I report a falaj issue?

You can report a falaj issue through the "Report Issue" feature in your account. Provide details about the problem, location, and any relevant information to expedite the resolution process.

5. How is my data protected?

We take data security seriously. We employ encryption, access controls, and regular security audits to protect your information. You can learn more in our Privacy Policy.

6. How can I contact support?

If you need assistance or have further questions, please reach out to our support team at support@aflaj.online.
